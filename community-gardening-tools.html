ArtsPay

The ArtsPay Foundation is an independent, artist-led charitable foundation with a mission to support independent artists and small arts organisations across Australia. Every time a customer makes a purchase at a business using ArtsPay - either in-store or online - ArtsPay uses the payment fees to support the arts.
